What is Udgam?

Udgam is the annual entrepreneurship summit of IIT Guwahati where people interested in entrepreneurship meet, discuss, explore, share, debate, and have fun with an aim to spread entrepreneurship across the country and ignite the flame within.

Udgam is
going Virtual

this year!

Udgam, every year, is hosted offline in our IIT-G campus. This year, however, we are riding with the wave of change and taking Udgam the virtual way.

However, this does not mean any less. Udgam this year promises more speakers, more ideas, more discussion, more prizes and more knowledge.

All from the comfort of your homes - We will be making sure you enjoy the event in its entirety.
